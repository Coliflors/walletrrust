Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Request Cards (the “Site”), accessible at www.requestcards.com, handles information when you visit and use its wallet connection feature.

1. Who we are

Request Cards is an informational static website about crypto-friendly card products. It is operated as a personal project. Contact: hello@requestcards.com.

2. What information we collect

The Site is intentionally minimal. We do not collect, store or sell personal data. Specifically:

  • No accounts. There is no registration, login or password.
  • No cookies for tracking. The Site does not use third-party analytics, advertising or fingerprinting cookies.
  • No KYC. We never request name, address, ID documents or financial information.
  • Wallet address. When you click “Connect wallet”, your wallet provider (Trust Wallet, MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.) shares your public blockchain address and current chain ID with the Site. This information is read-only, stays in your browser and is not transmitted to any server we control.
  • Server logs. Our hosting provider (Vercel) may collect standard HTTP access logs (IP address, user-agent, requested URL, timestamp) for security and abuse prevention, in accordance with its own privacy practices.

3. Wallet connection scope

The wallet connection on this Site is read-only. The Site:

  • Does not request signatures.
  • Does not request token approvals.
  • Does not submit on-chain transactions on your behalf.
  • Does not have access to your seed phrase, private keys, or funds.

You can disconnect your wallet at any time by clicking “Disconnect” in the Site, or by revoking the session from your wallet app.

4. Third-party services

The Site relies on the following third-party infrastructure. Each operates under its own privacy policy:

  • Vercel — static hosting and CDN.
  • Reown / WalletConnect — relayer for the WalletConnect protocol.
  • Public RPC endpoints used by your wallet to read blockchain state.

5. Children

The Site is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly process information from them.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the Site after a change constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

7. Contact

Questions or requests related to this policy can be sent to hello@requestcards.com.
